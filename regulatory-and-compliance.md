# Regulatory and Compliance

Regulatory compliance is presented as a strategic priority for Tetreum.

The whitepaper states that the project intends to operate with proactive legal guidance and evolving compliance policies as the regulatory environment develops.

## Topics referenced in the source

- KYC and AML processes
- Counter-terrorism financing requirements
- SEC / Howey Test considerations
- EU MiCA alignment
- FinCEN guidance for digital asset businesses

## Ongoing commitments listed

- Transparent legal disclosures
- Periodic compliance reviews
- Third-party legal and policy guidance

The stated philosophy is to foster innovation while operating within the law.
