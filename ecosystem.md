# Tetreum Ecosystem

Tetreum is designed to support a broad ecosystem of developers, businesses, and users.

## Areas highlighted in the source

- DeFi
- NFTs
- Gaming
- Enterprise systems

## Ecosystem support elements

- Developer tools and documentation
- Partnership and builder programs
- Grants and technical support
- Community participation through hackathons, bounties, and DAOs

The ecosystem is positioned as a central pillar of long-term adoption.
