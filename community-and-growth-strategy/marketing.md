# Marketing

The marketing strategy is designed to educate, onboard, and retain users while expanding global awareness of the protocol.

## Marketing pillars listed in the source

- Digital outreach across Google, X, YouTube, and Telegram
- Educational campaigns such as explainers, technical sessions, and tutorials
- Influencer and KOL collaborations
- Ongoing blog and content production
- Conference, summit, and hackathon participation

## Official channels listed in the source

- X / Twitter
- Telegram
- Medium
- GitHub
- Linktree

The whitepaper emphasizes ethical and community-aligned growth rather than purely speculative promotion.
