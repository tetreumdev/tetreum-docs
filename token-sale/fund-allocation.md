# Fund Allocation

The token sale section of the source provides the following high-level sale allocation details.

## Sale allocation

- **Total TET supply:** 1,000,000,000 TET
- **Public sale allocation:** 200,000,000 TET
- **Public sale share:** 20% of total supply

The remaining supply is described as being reserved for ecosystem growth, treasury, liquidity, team, partnerships, and long-term development.
