# Payments

Tetreum's low-latency and low-fee design is positioned as a strong fit for everyday digital payments.

## Example benefits listed in the source

- Instant settlements in approximately 5 seconds
- Cross-border transfers without intermediaries
- Micropayments enabled by negligible gas costs

## Example application areas

- Decentralized e-commerce
- Subscription billing
- Digital services and pay-per-use models
