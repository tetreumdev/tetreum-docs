# Risk Factors

The source identifies several risk categories.

## Regulatory uncertainty

Digital asset regulation continues to evolve and may affect the availability or operation of Tetreum services.

## Smart contract vulnerabilities

Audits can reduce risk, but undetected bugs may still exist.

## Market volatility

TET may be affected by crypto market cycles, speculation, and macroeconomic conditions.

## Technological risks

Consensus, infrastructure, or interoperability issues could disrupt operations.

## Operational risks

Team changes, strategic mistakes, or poor resource allocation could slow progress.
