# Market Opportunity

The whitepaper frames Tetreum as an infrastructure play within a rapidly expanding global blockchain market.

It argues that growth in institutional adoption, DeFi, and blockchain integration across industries is creating demand for networks that are more usable, affordable, and developer-friendly.

## Why Tetreum believes the opportunity exists

- Many existing networks remain difficult to use at scale
- High fees and poor UX still slow mainstream adoption
- Developers need predictable infrastructure for dApps
- Governance-driven ecosystems need better base layers

Tetreum positions itself as a launchpad for staking products, governance systems, DeFi projects, and cross-chain applications that want performance without the complexity of legacy chains.
