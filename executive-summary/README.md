# Executive Summary

Tetreum is positioned as a next-generation blockchain platform focused on scalability, speed, and real-world utility. The whitepaper presents Tetreum as a Proof of Authority network with fast finality, low operational overhead, and full EVM compatibility.

The ecosystem is designed around a fixed 1,000,000,000 TET supply and aims to support validators, users, developers, staking products, DAO governance, and application growth.

## Core themes

- High-speed and low-cost transactions
- Sustainable PoA-based validation
- Native staking and reward mechanisms
- DAO governance capabilities
- EVM compatibility for Ethereum developers

This section serves as the high-level introduction to the protocol and the role TET plays in the network.
