# Voting System

Tetreum describes a token-weighted voting model in which staked TET determines influence in governance decisions.

## Rules described in the source

- `1 TET = 1 vote` at the current design stage
- Governance phases include proposal, discussion, voting, and execution
- Certain actions may require quorum or supermajority thresholds
- Accepted proposals may be enforced automatically through DAO contracts

The system is intended to keep governance decentralized, transparent, and enforceable on-chain.
